\part{Elements of a Complex Document}
\label{part:elements}

Part~\ref{part:elements}, {\it\nameref{part:elements}}, describes the major
components of \TeX\ involved in producing a non-trivial document.  It
discusses how to use different font technologies, how to incorporate pictures
and figures into the document, how to typeset non-English languages,
and how to maintain complex structures like
indexes and bibliographies.  This section also explains the preview and
printing processes in detail.
